Environmental impact

Thanks to solar energy, among others, we are achieving a better conservation of our planet by reducing gtCO2e. This is partly a consequence of the reduced need to generate electricity by non-renewable means and the decrease in tree felling associated with the previous point.

Investing in domestic solar energy will not only have long term benefits, it will also help the environment and will be a step towards a more sustainable world.
