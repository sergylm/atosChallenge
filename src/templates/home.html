About us

We are two software engineering students trying to change our planet for a better future. Every year the carbon footprint is increasing and our planet is suffocating little by little, year after year. Reducing the carbon footprint is the main goal of our project and our motivation. We aim to reduce greenhouse gas emissions through the widespread use of domestic solar panels, by providing our users with useful tools to determine the feasibility of using

solar panels. In this way, we hope that renewable energies will take a step ahead of the others, reducing the carbon footprint produced by non-renewable energy organisations.
